     Reconnaisance of unusual sighting in area WH by a patrol of the 2nd Underworld: 
      Wizard Khojah (Power-4) offered only a portion of his magic to the patrol's efforts as he
     decided to cast a guarded attack spell and protect himself from the unknown. A patrol
     consisting of Power-4 Wizard Khojah and a dozen hand-picked troops rode ahead to
     investigate the sighting. It had been decided that there would be no plan to retreat. The
     patrol was willing to take great risks to uncover what might be secreted ahead. Without a
     leader present, the force was at a disadvantage. At this elevation snow maintains a
     constant presence. occasionally is heard and even seen, avalanches taking sections of the
     snow and even portions of the mountain hurling down into the valleys below. Ahead is a
     strange, elevated, flat, dry area, upon which rests a small, mausoleum-like structure.
     Thick cobwebs cover its stone entrance, and snakes slither about, hissing a warning. The
     patrol is able to pull open the entrance, and explore the chambers within. Inside are
     heroic visages of good overcoming evil, inscribed on the walls. Suddenly, as if from the
     painting itself, blending with the snow and ice surroundings of the cave, a huge yeti
     initiates conflict with the group by moving with great speed despite its size. A deadly
     adversary for the surprised patrol which requires some time to begin their counterattack.
     Within a few seconds 3 soldiers died at the hand of this evil opponent. Now the patrol's
     leaders fought with their full abilities. The conflict was furious. The snow yeti proved
     to be a very formidable opponent. The contest raged on, its outcome in doubt.  The contest
     raged on, it's outcome in doubt. The next few minutes saw 5 warriors perish. Hope ran
     high, though, as the foe was weakened. Wizard Khojah cunningly employed his magic against
     the opponent. The foe reeled from the effect. The yeti proved too worthy of a foe and a
     retreat was sounded. The patrol made a hasty retreat with it's surviving members,
     reporting heavy-hearted back to the group.
