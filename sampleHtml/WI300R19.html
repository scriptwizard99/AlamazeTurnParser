     Reconnaisance of unusual sighting in area AH by a patrol of the 2nd Witchlord: 
      Wizard Ry Vor (Power-7) offered his full magical potential to the patrol's efforts
     knowing no harm will come from being a lich. Wizard Darco (Power-5) offered only a portion
     of his magic to the patrol's efforts as he decided to cast a guarded attack spell and
     protect himself from the unknown. Wizard Grolig (Power-5) offered only a portion of his
     magic to the patrol's efforts as he decided to cast a guarded attack spell and protect
     himself from the unknown. A patrol consisting of Captain I Hurin, Captain Stanz, Power-7
     Wizard Ry Vor, Power-5 Wizard Darco, Power-5 Wizard Grolig and a dozen hand-picked troops
     rode ahead to investigate the sighting. It had been decided that there would be no plan to
     retreat. The patrol was willing to take great risks to uncover what might be secreted
     ahead. After a lull in the wind, the ship containing the crew and a handful of armed
     guards begins to drift upon the sea due to the strong current and the craft traveled some
     distance away from its original path. Suddenly a huge serpent head rises from the water
     near our hull and hisses angerly at the crew, making them terrified of the massive
     creature. Upon seeing this monstrosity, the patrol leaped into action to protect the ship
     and her crew. in the first moments of battle, 4 warriors were slain by this fiendish
     enemy. The patrol then attacked with fury. The conflict was hectic, with one powerful snap
     of the creature's jaws, the serpent crushed the mast of the ship into two. the crew became
     desperate, the heroes attacked with greater furiosity than ever to save the ship or all
     will be lost. Waving in the wind, the Standard of Valor bolstered the Witchlord patrol
     with hope. By raising Elan, the Flaming Sword of the North, the Captain Hurin infused his
     patrol with boldness and propelled them forward.  Witchlord leader Captain I Hurin was
     slain in battle. A new leader, Captain Pierznok has emerged as a courageous leader of much
     promise. The contest raged on, it's outcome in doubt. The next few minutes saw 5 warriors
     perish. Hope ran high, though, as the foe was weakened. Wizard Grolig cast his magic that
     severely hampered the foe. When the battle took a turn for the worst, Wizard Ry Vor
     stepped up and employed his magic to distract the foe long enough for the patrol to regain
     their ground. Wizard Darco cunningly employed his magic against the opponent. The foe
     reeled from the effect. The battle went on for what seemed like hours exhausting all in
     the grueling confrontation, finally the massive serpent's head fell upon the ship and
     moved no more. The patrol had prevailed. Upon exploring the area further, the patrol finds
     an elegant pouch with a magical ring inside. The Ring of Power is a magical artifact which
     will immediately raise the power of the highest ranking wizard in the group and will act
     as magical protection for him, greatly reducing the chance of being slain in combat by
     normal means or by assassin. As the serpent's body was split open, a huge diamond is found
     worth 24,000 gold. The Key of the Maker, having served it's purpose disappears.
