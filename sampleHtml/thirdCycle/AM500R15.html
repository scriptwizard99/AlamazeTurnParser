     Reports of our Military engagements:

     Reconnaisance of unusual sighting in area UR by a patrol of the 2nd Amazons: 
      Wizard Dragonpass (Power-4) offered only a portion of his magic to the patrol's efforts
     as he decided to cast a guarded attack spell and protect himself from the unknown. Wizard
     Freehaven (Power-4) decided to stay behind in camp and will not be a factor in assisting
     the patrol. Wizard Cunnafor (Power-1) decided to stay behind in camp and will not be a
     factor in assisting the patrol. A patrol consisting of Commander Chaos, Major General
     Turingoth, Centurion Tanabor, Power-4 Wizard Dragonpass and a dozen hand-picked troops
     rode ahead to investigate the sighting. It had been decided that there would be no plan to
     retreat. The patrol was willing to take great risks to uncover what might be secreted
     ahead. Appearing strangely out of place is a black monolithic structure of 40' per side
     and 40' height built from materials not found in this area. runes on one side read
     "unending pain and inexplicable horror await any heretic who dares to enter". Undaunted,
     the patrol go in. As each member of the group pass the threshold, images of various
     terrors form in their minds and a cold sweat appears on their brow. Somehow the monolith
     warps space, as the interior appears many times larger than the 40' exterior. The interior
     has its own dim light, from an unknown source. The patrol continues ahead. materializing
     directly in front of the group is the obscure image of a ghost. moving mist-like toward
     the startled patrol this evil aberration seeks to embrace each living being. The
     horrifying experience of seeing fellow members age dramatically, and even die through that
     process, will forever linger in the minds and dreams of the patrol. By raising the Great
     Axe of Hurblind Commander Chaos infused his patrol with boldness and propelled them
     forward.  In a setback, the Amazons Commander Chaos perished. Turingoth impressed and has
     been recognized as a Commander. Surpassing expectations was Tanabor, who again has won
     further glory. He is now a Captain. In the heroic tradition of the Amazons kingdom, a
     glorius leader has distinguished himself in this engagement. He is Centurion Xhose. The
     battle raged on, furiously contested for what seemed like hours. The next few minutes saw
     2 soldiers lose their lives, but the enemy had been weakened. Fighting bravely in the
     battle was Captain Tanabor. Never the less, the remaining members of the stalwart compose
     themselves and move tediously to unearth the costly rarity the ghost was guarding. With
     the battle over, the group finds an interesting object nearby. The Palantir Crisnia allows
     a king to view an area as if an agent were located there: use Order #972, put the short
     name in Column "A", area to be viewed in Column "B". The impressive foe subdued, the
     surviving patrol members find valuables worth 9,000 gold.

     Assault by the 3rd Amazons army group on the Sacred Order village of Omni, located in area
     IL of Amberland: It was already known that Omni had as its main defensive structure a
     surrounding wall, containing several unreinforced gates. When the sun had risen to its
     highest point, Lord Commander Goodkind commanded his 3rd Amazons army group to engage the
     village. As the attackers swept over the flat grasslands, the battle began. In the initial
     minutes of battle, defending missilers were too few to concern the determined attackers.
     The Amazons ranks caused tremendous casualties upon the village's defenders. Within a few
     hours, the village defenses were obliterated and an unconditional surrender was offered.
     The Amazons banner now waves nobly in Omni! Amazons casualties were put at 1%. All Amazons
     leaders survived the battle. Impressing his peers was Goodkind, who again has
     distinguished himself. He is now a Warlord. The troops are eager for more victories:
     morale is up!
