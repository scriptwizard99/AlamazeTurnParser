     Diplomacy            This is a full diplomacy game where all communications are encouraged among
                          the players throughout the game.

     Exploratory          This is an exploratory game, no divinations (either by priestess or consul)
                          are permitted until turn 4.

     Royal Palace         There are some restrictions in acquiring a high priestess in the game.
                          First, a temple is required to be constructed at the population center
                          before a noble maiden may be hired (use Order #611 located under the
                          Kingdom menu as a Popcenter action). A noble maiden may then be hired at
                          the temple's location with Order #510 (located under the Kingdom menu as a
                          Miscellaneous action). The orders to create a temple or a noble maiden may
                          be issued on any turn. To advance a noble maiden into becoming a high
                          priestess, the maiden must successfully pass her test for the gift with
                          Order #509 (located under the Divine menu as a Miscellaneous action)
                          however the test may only be performed on turn 3 or later.

     Royal Palace         Issue Order #990 (located under the Political menu as a King action) to
                          change your ruler's name or gender on turn 1. Since this order will be
                          executed at the end of the turn, specify the original ruler's name when
                          issuing a turn 1 order that requires your ruler's abbreviation (e.g.,
                          increase influence or raise regional reaction). You may start using the new
                          name with your turn 2 orders.

     Royal Palace         If you wish to customize your kingdom, issue Order #11 on turns 1, 2, or 3
                          (located under the Other menu as a Miscellaneous action). Keep in mind that
                          if you choose a political figure (e.g., Baron) as an award and wish to
                          change your ruler's name on turn 1 with Order #990, the two may conflict
                          with each other regarding duplicate emissary names/abbreviations so
                          consider issuing Order #11 on turn 2 or 3 instead. Otherwise, you may issue
                          Order #11 on turn 1 to benefit from it as early as possible.
